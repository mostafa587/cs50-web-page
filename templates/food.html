1. TAMIYA

Tamiya (Egyptian Falafel) is the most popular fast food to try in Egypt. Tamiya is made of cooked fava beans putting together in a dough and later fried. You can find Tamiya or Egyptian Falafel as street food almost everywhere. You can eat with bread or salad and Egyptian Falafel is a perfect vegetarian food in Egypt.

3. Mulukhiyah

Molokhia is made from Molokhia leaves after they are finely chopped with garlic and coriander. The leaves are then boiled with chunks of meat, such as boneless chicken, lamb or rabbit. Molokhia is served with rice or Egyptian flatbread.
